THE STORY

Memoreez is a quick and easy way for family, friends and acquaintances to leave you pictures, contact information and a personal messages all in one place at your event to view later and share with anyone. Think of it as your digital guestbook that can be used at weddings, holiday parties, company events or anywhere you want. Creating a Memoreez event is as easy as entering your name, event type, and email address. We’ll do all the heavy lifting and set it up.
